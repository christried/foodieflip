About Foodie Flip

Why all of this?

Hey there! I'm Chris and I'm honestly not motivated enough to prompt any AI to write this for me so I'll do it myself. The whole app is my first (hopefully) maintained project that I'll continue to use to practice web app development. If you'll consider using Foodie Flip regularly, be prepared for it to break from time to time. In exchange I'll promise to take constructive criticism to heart and keep improving the experience on Foodie Flip - for all Foodies including myself. To be completely honest I started Foodie Flip because I was lacking a resource to get cooking inspiration that is 100% vegan and I am too lazy to think of stuff myself most of the time.

Why vegan?

Because it's the right thing to do. Nothing else on this site will give away that there's never gonna be any animal abuse showing on Foodie Flip but I thought you may as well be interested in this if you click on this link. This app is about good food and great times so I don't see much use in explaining basics and/or sad facts. Instead please check out additional ressources if you're interested in learning about the truth behind everything non-vegan.
